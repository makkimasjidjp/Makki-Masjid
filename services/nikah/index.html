Nikah Services

Marriage (Nikah) is a sacred bond in Islam. Makki Masjid Tokyo provides guidance and facilitation for Islamic marriages in accordance with Shariah principles.

Requirements for Nikah
  • Consent of both bride and groom
  • Presence of a Wali (guardian) for the bride
  • At least two Muslim witnesses
  • Agreement on Mahr (dowry)
  • Both parties must be Muslim
How to Proceed

To arrange a Nikah at Makki Masjid, please contact us in advance to discuss requirements, availability, and the process. We encourage families to meet with the Imam beforehand.
