Welcome to Makki Masjid

Makki Masjid Tokyo is a community-based mosque in Katsushika City dedicated to serving the spiritual and social needs of Muslims in Tokyo. We strive to strengthen faith, build community bonds, and offer a welcoming place for worship and learning.

  • Five daily prayers in congregation
  • Friday (Jumu'ah) prayer every week
  • Islamic lectures and community gatherings
  • Islamic education through Makki Gakuin
  • Open to all Muslims in Tokyo and visitors
