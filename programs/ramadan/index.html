Ramadan at Makki Masjid

The blessed month of Ramadan is a time of increased worship, reflection, and community. Makki Masjid organizes special programs to help the community make the most of this sacred month.

Taraweeh Prayer

Nightly Taraweeh prayers after Isha throughout Ramadan. Full Quran completion in 30 nights.

Iftar Gatherings

Community Iftar dinners on selected nights. All Muslims welcome. Please contact us for schedule.

Laylat al-Qadr

Special programs during the last 10 nights of Ramadan including night prayers and Quran recitation.

Eid al-Fitr

Eid prayer and community celebration. Details announced closer to the date.

Ramadan 2025 / 1446 AH

Exact dates will be confirmed by moon sighting. Schedule announcement will be posted in Announcements.
